---
title_en: Fluoxetine
title_zh: 氟西汀
summary_en: SSRI antidepressant commonly used for depression and anxiety disorders.
summary_zh: 常用于抑郁和焦虑障碍的选择性 5-羟色胺再摄取抑制剂。
drug_class_en: Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor (SSRI)
drug_class_zh: 选择性 5-羟色胺再摄取抑制剂（SSRI）
mechanism_en: |
  Selectively inhibits presynaptic serotonin reuptake transporter (SERT), increasing serotonin concentration in the synaptic cleft.
mechanism_zh: |
  选择性抑制突触前膜 5-羟色胺转运体（SERT），增加突触间隙中的 5-羟色胺浓度。
pharmacokinetics_en: |
  - Oral bioavailability is good.
  - Long half-life (parent drug about 2-4 days; active metabolite norfluoxetine about 7-15 days).
  - Hepatic metabolism mainly through CYP2D6.
pharmacokinetics_zh: |
  - 口服生物利用度较好。
  - 半衰期较长（母药约 2-4 天；活性代谢物去甲氟西汀约 7-15 天）。
  - 主要经肝脏 CYP2D6 代谢。
indications_en: |
  - Major depressive disorder
  - Obsessive-compulsive disorder
  - Panic disorder
  - Bulimia nervosa
indications_zh: |
  - 重性抑郁障碍
  - 强迫障碍
  - 惊恐障碍
  - 神经性贪食
side_effects_en: |
  - Nausea, insomnia, anxiety, headache
  - Sexual dysfunction
  - Risk of serotonin syndrome when combined with serotonergic drugs
side_effects_zh: |
  - 恶心、失眠、焦虑、头痛
  - 性功能障碍
  - 与其他促 5-羟色胺药物联用时有 5-羟色胺综合征风险
contraindications_en: |
  - Concurrent MAOI use or within 14 days of MAOI discontinuation
  - Known hypersensitivity
contraindications_zh: |
  - 与 MAOI 联用，或停用 MAOI 未满 14 天
  - 已知对本药过敏
drug_interactions_en: |
  - Inhibits CYP2D6 and can increase levels of some antipsychotics and beta blockers.
  - Combined use with linezolid, tramadol, or triptans increases serotonin toxicity risk.
drug_interactions_zh: |
  - 抑制 CYP2D6，可升高部分抗精神病药和 β 受体阻滞剂浓度。
  - 与利奈唑胺、曲马多、曲坦类联用会增加 5-羟色胺毒性风险。
exam_notes_en: |
  Frequently tested point: long half-life compared with many other SSRIs; lower discontinuation syndrome risk.
exam_notes_zh: |
  常考点：相较多数 SSRI 半衰期更长，停药综合征风险相对较低。
personal_notes_en: |
  Professor emphasized that washout period matters before switching to MAOI because of norfluoxetine persistence.
personal_notes_zh: |
  老师强调与 MAOI 切换时要注意洗脱期，因为去甲氟西汀持续时间较长。
