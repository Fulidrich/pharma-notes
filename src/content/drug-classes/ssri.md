title_zh: SSRI 类药物
summary_en: First-line antidepressant class for depression and anxiety disorders.
summary_zh: 抑郁和焦虑障碍常用的一线抗抑郁药物类别。
body_en: |
  # Overview
  SSRIs block serotonin reuptake and increase synaptic serotonin activity.

  # Core Clinical Pearls
  - Usually safer in overdose than tricyclic antidepressants.
  - Typical adverse effects include gastrointestinal symptoms and sexual dysfunction.

  # Representative Drugs
  - Fluoxetine
  - Sertraline
  - Escitalopram
body_zh: |
  # 概述
  SSRI 通过抑制 5-羟色胺再摄取来增强突触间 5-羟色胺信号。

  # 临床要点
  - 过量使用时通常比三环类抗抑郁药更安全。
  - 常见不良反应包括胃肠道症状和性功能障碍。

  # 代表药物
  - 氟西汀
  - 舍曲林
  - 艾司西酞普兰
